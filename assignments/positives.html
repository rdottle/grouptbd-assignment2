What’s great about this project is that you can see how two people took the same theme and created totally different visualizations. Formatted like a diary entry, complete with sketches and links to the datasets, over the course of the project to give a sense of the process. They show us sketches of different avenues that they explored.

Who’s speaking in middle earth?: Film flowers:

This narrative diary format gives them the opportunity to give the rationale for the decisions they make.

The list of characters in the film included in this spreadsheet is more than the amount she included in her data sketch. She whittled down the list of characters and focused on the main group of characters who are the driving force for the plot of the three films. The last variable in her data sketch is the location that these characters were in the film where they spoke the lines. This is not included in the .tsv file.
